✓ Effective Potential Field Engine (EPFE)
  - SOS polynomial parameterization: V_eff(Q) = p(Q)^T P p(Q)
  - Unique global attractor Q* guaranteed
  - No local minima (false attractors)

✓ Kuramoto Phase Synchronization Layer (KPSL)
  - Modified Kuramoto: dθ_i/dt = ω_i + (K/N) Σ sin(θ_j - θ_i)
  - Critical coupling threshold: K_c = 2Δ
  - Overcritical operation: K = 3K_c

✓ Stability Certification
  - Jacobian eigenvalue analysis: Re(λ_i) < -σ_min < 0
  - Exponential convergence rate guaranteed
  - Basin of attraction characterized by sublevel sets
